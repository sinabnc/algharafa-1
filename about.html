About Us

CHAIRMAN'S MESSAGE

As we anticipate the future and draw inspiration from our ancestors’ vision for beloved Qatar, Al-Gharafa Trading and Contracting is dedicated to an exceptional plan that defines the Qatari identity. Our commitment reflects a passion for delivering superior services and solutions in contracting and construction. While client satisfaction is a common goal, we aim higher by positioning ourselves as partners striving for excellence and leadership. To achieve this, we have established quality, occupational safety, and speed of accomplishment as standards. These are realized through expert teams and meticulous plans, executed with full awareness and unwavering commitment at every stage. We eagerly anticipate strengthening relationships and forging partnerships with our clients to fulfill our vision and strategic objectives. Thank you for your trust. We are confident that your experience with us will be exceptional and unique.

Our History

Crafting structures that last a lifetime

Al Gharafa Trading and Contracting Co. W.L.L (AGTC) is 100% Qatari national company owned by Mr. Sheikh Essa Bin Hamad Bin Jassim Bin Thani Al Thani and registered in Doha, The establishment of AGTC since 2007 is a natural progression of the Essa Bin Hamad Company (EBH Co.) policy of controlled growth and diversification.

Our Mission

Al Gharafa Trading and Contracting Co. W.L.L is dedicated to providing value added general contracting services to its Client via successful partnership with them throughout the whole construction process, and consult technological and professional improvement of its team. .

Our Vision

Our vision is to maintain our growth as one of the leading contracting companies in the State of Qatar by delivering unique projects for exceptional clients and this in line with Qatar 2030 vision. In that regard, AGTC is committed to delivering high quality.

Our Values

Integrity – Quality – Customer Satisfaction – Health & Safety – Reliability – Teamwork – Innovation – Environmental Responsibility – Community Involvement – Financial Responsibility.
